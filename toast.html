TOAST

Description

Here is a quick and easy way to make warm delicios toast for breakfast! This simple recipe can be used at home, for a sit down meal or a quick on the go meal before you head off

Ingredients

  • one sliced bread
  • dime size of butter
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Place sliced bread in toaster and toast until bread begings to carmelize
  2. Remove from toasted and place on plate.
  3. Spread butter over toast
  4. Sprinkle pinch of salt over butter toast.

ENJOY!
