Pancakes

Description

A flat, round, starch-based cake, often cooked on a griddle, that serves as a staple breakfast food worldwide. Typically made from a batter of flour, eggs, and milk, they are fried in butter or oil until golden brown. Pancakes range from thin, delicate crepes to thick, fluffy, leavened cakes topped with syrup or fruits

Ingredients

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • milk
  • egg
  • butter (or oil)

Steps

  1. whisk 1.5 cups flour
  2. 1 tbsp sugar
  3. 1 tbsp baking powder
  4. a pinch of salt
  5. Create a well and mix in 1.25 cups milk
  6. 1 egg, and 3 tbsp oil or melted butter until just combined
  7. just combined. Cook on a buttered skillet over medium heat, flipping when bubbles form and edges dry, until golden brown
