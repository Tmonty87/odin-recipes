Hotdog!

Description

A hot dog is a fully cooked, cured, and emulsified sausage—typically pork, beef, chicken, or turkey—served hot in a partially sliced bun. Often called a frankfurter or wiener, it is seasoned with spices like garlic and paprika and commonly topped with mustard, ketchup, onions, or relish.

Ingredients

  • high-quality all-beef franks
  • classic yellow mustard
  • diced onions
  • A soft bun
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grill or pan-fry a high-quality beef frankfurter until charred
  2. toast a top-split bun with butter
  3. top with yellow mustard and finely diced white onions
